Biography

Echoes of Fleetwood Mac, Soul of Kath May

Nelson-based singer-songwriter Kath May has always been under the spell of Fleetwood Mac's timeless harmonies and poetic lyrics. With a voice that echoes the haunting beauty of Stevie Nicks, Kath weaves her own magic into the band's beloved classics. A lifelong musician, Kath cut her teeth on the Nelson music scene, performing in various bands and collaborations before discovering her true calling as a Fleetwood Mac interpreter. Her mesmerizing live shows have captivated audiences across the South Island, transporting them to a world of dreams, love, and mystique. With a setlist that spans Fleetwood Mac's iconic discography, from "Rhiannon" to "Landslide", Kath May's performances are a must-experience for fans of the legendary band.
